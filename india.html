India

India is home to numerous iconic travel destinations. The Taj Mahal in Agra, an epitome of love, and Jaipur’s grand palaces showcase its architectural splendor. The serene backwaters of Kerala, the spiritual ghats of Varanasi, and the vibrant streets of Delhi offer varied experiences. The Himalayan regions, including Ladakh and Himachal Pradesh, attract adventure seekers. India’s diversity, from the deserts of Rajasthan to the lush tea gardens of Assam, makes it a captivating travel destination.
