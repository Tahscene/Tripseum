Greece

Explore ancient ruins like the Parthenon in Athens and the Palace of Knossos in Crete, where myths and legends come to life. Relax on stunning beaches with crystal-clear waters in Santorini and Mykonos, enjoying breathtaking sunsets. Savor delectable Greek cuisine, from savory moussaka to fresh seafood. Wander through charming villages with whitewashed houses and vibrant blue domes. Experience the warm hospitality of the locals, immerse yourself in traditional music and dance, and discover the rich heritage that makes Greece a timeless and unforgettable destination.
