Bangladesh

From the bustling streets of Dhaka to the serene beaches of Cox's Bazar, the world's longest natural sea beach, the country boasts diverse attractions. Explore the Sundarbans, home to the majestic Bengal tiger and a UNESCO World Heritage site. Visit the ancient city of Bagerhat, with its historic mosques, or the tea gardens of Sylhet for a tranquil escape. Enjoy the warm hospitality of the Bangladeshi people, savoring delicious local cuisine.
